Garlic Parmesan Shrimp

Prep Details

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 4

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Nonstick cooking spray
  • 1 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • 11/2 teaspoons of garlic powder
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted, or more to taste

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven at 350oF (1750C). Spray a 9x13 inch glass casserole dish with nonstick pan spray

Step 2

Combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley and garlic powder in a large resealable palstic bag. Seal and shake to mix ingredients together. Add shrimp and shake to coat.

Step 3

Lay shrimp flat in the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle with any bread crumb mixture remaining in the bag. Drizzle melted butter over the top.

Step 4

Bake in the preheated oven until starting to crisp, 15-20 minutes. Switch to broil setting until topping is light brown, 2 to 3 minutes.
